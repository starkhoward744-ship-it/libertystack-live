08 / FAQ

Questions,
answered.

How long does a website project take?+

Focused websites typically take 3–6 weeks. Larger platforms and automation systems are scoped around complexity, integrations and approval speed.

Do you use templates?+

We may use proven technical foundations, but the strategy, interface, content hierarchy and visual system are shaped around your business.

Can you improve an existing website?+

Yes. We can redesign the complete experience or improve high-impact pages, conversion paths, performance and automation in phases.

Can you guarantee Lighthouse 95+?+

We engineer toward 95+ using lightweight assets, semantic code and careful loading. Scores can vary by hosting, third-party scripts, device and network conditions.

What happens after launch?+

You receive handover guidance, deployment support and an optional optimization plan for ongoing content, experiments and automation.
